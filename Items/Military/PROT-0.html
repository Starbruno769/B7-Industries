PROT-0

Designation: PROT-0

Type: Visor

Role: Face Protection

Info:

The PROT-0 Visor is a sleek, customizable visor designed for a low profile but high protection.

It comes with a fully functional, customizable HUD that shows the user information received from their equipment, along with an extendable antenna and headset to allow communication among teammates.

Like the UNDR Suit, PROT-0 Visors are not mass produced. They are instead made individually for each soldier to maintain low profile.

They are available as either a standalone visor or as a fully covered helmet, depending on the user's preference.

The composite visor itself is very sturdy and bulletproof, ensuring the protection of the wearer.

It is common among soldiers to paint designs on the visors, which is condoned by B7.
