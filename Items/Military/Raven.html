< R453 <

Designation: R453

Type: Air Drone

Role: Scouting

Nickname: "Raven"

Info:

The R453 is a small, quick propeller drone used by scouts.

It's commonly used for recon of an area, and comes with a tagging system that displays spotted enemies on the PROT-0 Visor's HUD.

It obtained its nickname from folklore and mythology, due to ravens commonly being considered "all seeing".

The small size allows it to be fired from an arm mounted launcher, or holstered on a BacKit.
