< Goldie Smiths

Name: Goldie Smiths

Species: Golden Retriever

Specialization: Research and Development

Info:

Goldie Smiths is the sweet but stern head of the R&D Division. She manages projects and monitors researchers, ensuring maximum safety and efficiency. She's known to have "a heart as a golden as her fur", having a positive reputation among members of B7. Her management skills and expertise in the sciences are almost unparalleled, making her invaluable in her division.
