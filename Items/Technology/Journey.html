Journey

Name: "Journey"

Type: Artificial Intelligence

Role: Digital Assistance

Info:

Journey is an AI developed by B7 as a digital assistant.

She runs most of B7 Industries' facilities due to being part of a specialized network. She also runs in every UNDR Suit and PROT-0 Visor, analyzing data from equipment and assisting the team in completing their tasks efficiently. She was developed with a snarky personality, so expect sarcasm. Don't worry, she has your best interests in mind.
