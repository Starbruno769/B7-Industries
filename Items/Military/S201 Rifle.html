Designation:S201

Role: Standard Assault Rifle

Info:

The S201 is an accurate, reliable assault rifle used by B7's assault teams.

It is given to every soldier in every team as a default weapon. There is not a single soldier without one in some form.
It's mass produced due to its relatively cheap cost, making it efficient in almost every way.
It has gained reputation among the soldiers due to its high customizability and modularity, meaning that every S201 is slightly different.
