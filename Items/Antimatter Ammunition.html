Antimatter Ammunition

WARNING: EXTREMELY EXPERIMENTAL! ONLY FOR USE IN EMERGENCIES!

Our Antimatter Ammunition uses specialized bullets with impact-deactivated containment fields containing high density Antimatter to bypass armor and walls.

There is very limited supply of it, and is only to be used when conventional means have no effect.

It is only supplied to the highest ranking teams, and even they only get a small amount of it.

What little supply we have is protected at all times to ensure it remains under our control, lest someone less lawful gets their hands on it.

It is extremely fragile. Handle with care.
