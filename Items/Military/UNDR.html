Designation: UNDR

Type: Undersuit

Role: Variable

Info:

The UNDR Suit is a light layer of armor that goes beneath any other equipment.

It has its own shield unit to provide protection in emergencies, keeping the wearer safe for a time.
The suit has multiple attachment points to allow for modules and equipment to be installed, improving its effectiveness.
It is standard issue, so almost all soldiers wear it. Even scientists may wear a light version if they feel the need for extra safety.
However, it is not mass produced. It is instead made individually for each user to ensure conformity and practicality.
