SIP™

SIP™ was a fundraising attempt by B7 that sold an energy drink made by R&D in order to help fund some of the projects.

It took a while to catch on, eventually becoming quite popular. A large number of people chose it instead of their original choice.
Unfortunately, it lost traction and is now replaced with other fundraising projects. However, it is still produced and sold, and we appreciate those who still take some time to have a SIP™.
